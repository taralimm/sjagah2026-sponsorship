Be Part of the St. Joseph’s Academy Grand Alumni Homecoming 2026

Dear Josephinians, Friends, and Supporters,

Mabuhay!

We are preparing for the St. Joseph’s Academy Grand Alumni Homecoming 2026, a special gathering where alumni from different batches will come together to reconnect, reminisce, and celebrate the school that shaped us.

As we organize this meaningful event, we are inviting those who would like to support the celebration through sponsorship or donation. Your contribution will help us cover the venue, food, program needs, decorations, tokens, and other important details that will make the day memorable for everyone.

Any remaining funds will go toward alumni initiatives or projects that benefit our alma mater. Every contribution, big or small, truly makes a difference in ensuring this homecoming is a celebration to remember.
